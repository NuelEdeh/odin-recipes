Feuilleté au jambon

Description

Feuilleté au jambon is a classic French pastry dish that translates to "puff pastry with ham." I learnt how to make this dish from my girlfriend. The dish is typically made with puff pastry. The pastry is filled with a mixture of diced mushrooms, ham, cheese, and béchamel sauce, which is a white sauce made from butter, flour, and milk. The filling is then wrapped in the puff pastry and baked until golden brown and crispy. The result is a savory and satisfying pastry that is perfect for a light lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of puff pasty
  • 1 cup of diced wild mushrooms
  • 1 cup diced ham
  • 1 cup of grated cheese (Cheddar or Gruyere)
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste
  • 1 egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Roll out the puff pastry on a floured surface to a thickness of about 1/4 inch (6mm).
  3. Cut the puff pastry into rectangles or squares of your desired size, usually 4-6 inches (10-15 cm) wide.
  4. Make the béchamel sauce
    1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk constantly for 2-3 minutes to make a roux.
    2. Slowly pour in the milk, whisking constantly, until the mixture is smooth and thick. Add salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste.
  5. Add the diced mushrooms, diced ham, and grated cheese to the béchamel sauce, and stir to combine.
  6. Spoon the ham and cheese mixture onto one half of each puff pastry rectangle, leaving a little bit of space around the edges.
  7. Brush the edges of the puff pastry with a beaten egg, and then fold the other half of the pastry over the filling. Use a fork to press the edges together and seal the pastry.
  8. Brush the tops of the pastries with more beaten egg to give them a golden brown color.
  9. Place the pastries on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the pastries are puffed and golden brown.
  10. Remove the feuilleté au jambon from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving.

    11. With a salad or soup, enjoy your delicious feuilleté au jambon. Bon appétit!
